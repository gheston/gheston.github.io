American Desert Grand Bike Tours

This map is of a fictional bike race through the American Southwest. I chose a southwest desert color scheme, and styled the terrain and land cover to make a semi-realistic base map. Each station is just a place I think is interestng. I'll be cheering on the racers from the comfort of my van.
