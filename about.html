Environmental and GIS Manager

2011-Present

I am the Environmental and GIS manager for Liberty Gold. In this role, I am responsible for a wide range of processes and tasks, including:

  • Overseeing the development of Exploration Plans of Operations with the U.S. Bureau of Land Management (BLM), U.S. Forest Service, and the States of Nevada, Idaho, and Utah.
  • Coordinating contractors conducting NEPA baseline studies and writing Environment Assessments
  • Maintaining positive relationships with stakeholders such as the BLM, Forest Service, State permitting agencies, NGOs, property owners, and contractors
  • Maintaining land tenure including mining claims and leases
  • Administering GIS software and databases, training and supporting staff
  • Developing a Stakeholder Engagement and Communication Plan and Environmental Management System
  • Developing a Sage-Grouse Conservation and Mitigation plan with consultants, NGOs, and agencies
  • Participating on the team that released Liberty Gold's innaugural ESG report
  • Editing the land/environmental/permitting sections for NI 43-101 technical reports

I am the 2022 recipient of the Utah Division of Oil, Gas, and Mining Environmental Excellence Award for Liberty Gold's innovative use of GIS to track reclamation that goes above and beyond industry standards and state regulations.
